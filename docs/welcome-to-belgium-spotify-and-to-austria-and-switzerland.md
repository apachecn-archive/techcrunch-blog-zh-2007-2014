# 欢迎来到比利时，Spotify。(以及奥地利和瑞士)

证实本周早些时候开始流传的[谣言](https://web.archive.org/web/20230205034436/http://paidcontent.org/article/419-spotify-expected-to-launch-in-more-countries-this-week/)，音乐流媒体现象 [Spotify](https://web.archive.org/web/20230205034436/http://www.crunchbase.com/company/spotify) 终于[来到我的祖国](https://web.archive.org/web/20230205034436/http://www.spotify.com/uk/blog/archives/2011/11/16/a-big-hello-to-belgium-and-switzerland/)[比利时](https://web.archive.org/web/20230205034436/http://www.spotify.com/be-nl/blog/archives/2011/11/16/a-big-hello-to-belgium-and-switzerland/)的之路，继昨天在奥地利首次亮相[之后，今天也将在瑞士](https://web.archive.org/web/20230205034436/http://www.spotify.com/uk/blog/archives/2011/11/15/hello-austria-spotify-here/)[推出](https://web.archive.org/web/20230205034436/http://open.spotify.com/user/spotify/playlist/5TgWvFTIhKilMNxPE3YT7b)。

这意味着 Spotify 现在可以在 12 个国家使用，其中 11 个在欧洲和美国。

目前没有其他的报告。如果你在奥地利、瑞士或比利时，查看 Spotify 的[定价计划](https://web.archive.org/web/20230205034436/http://www.spotify.com/uk/get-spotify/overview/)(从绝对免费到每月 9.99€不等)并享受。
