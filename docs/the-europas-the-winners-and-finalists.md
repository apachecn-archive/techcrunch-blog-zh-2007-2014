# Europas:获胜者和决赛选手| TechCrunch

> 原文：<https://web.archive.org/web/https://techcrunch.com/2009/07/09/the-europas-the-winners-and-finalists/>

为欧洲和 EMEA 科技公司举办的首届欧洲创业奖 2009 于昨晚(2009 年 7 月 9 日，星期四)在伦敦举行。请查看我们的[现场博客](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://uk.beta.techcrunch.com/2009/07/09/the-europas-liveblog-2009/)中的活动和后续报道[视频、图片和帖子](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://uk.beta.techcrunch.com/2009/07/10/the-europas-in-video-and-pictures/)。对于这些首届奖项，超过 400 名参赛者由行业投票产生，结果与来自欧洲各地的 19 名专家顾问的结果相结合。以下是每个类别的获奖者、高度赞扬者和决赛入围者。恭喜所有人！

非常感谢我们的赞助商对首届活动的支持:感谢 UKTI[对这些活动的赞助；](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://www.uktradeinvest.gov.uk/) [Viadeo](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://viadeo.com/) 赞助最佳设计类； [Bootlaw](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://www.bootlaw.com/) 赞助最佳自举创业公司； [Quick.tv](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://www.quick.tv/) 赞助最佳欧洲投资人；Zendesk 赞助最佳新创公司； [Moonfruit](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://www.moonfruit.com/) 赞助最佳社会创新；[纬度](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://www.latitudegroup.com/)和 [Parklane 香槟](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://www.parklanechampagne.co.uk/)为颁奖奖品；oneDrum 赞助酒会 [Mixcloud](https://web.archive.org/web/20230325205442/http://mixcloud.com/) 赞助 DJ。
