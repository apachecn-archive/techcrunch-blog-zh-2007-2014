萨克拉门托蜜蜂报[提到了](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208221636/http://www.sacbee.com/static/weblogs/capitolalertlatest/018071.html?mi_atom=Capitol%20Alert)我们的[正在进行的诉讼](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208221636/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2008/03/31/why-were-suing-facebook-for-25-million-in-statutory-damages/)与脸书和我们的[下一任](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208221636/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2008/12/22/facebook-chief-privacy-officer-chris-kelly-to-run-for-california-attorney-general/)司法部长、[克里斯·凯利](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208221636/http://www.crunchbase.com/person/chris-kelly)(这实际上是在愚人节晚些时候解决的)。

我以后要做的一件事就是回去明确标记愚人节的笑话。我很容易理解一个非技术记者是如何点击链接，看到链接，然后把它加在故事的结尾，制造这种混乱的。
