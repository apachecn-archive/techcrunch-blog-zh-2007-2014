# Personforce 推出移动求职平台，开发求职应用程序

Dice.com 最近发布的数据显示，技术类工作仍然很难找到。Dice [报道称](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208231948/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2009/07/02/dice-reports-murky-waters-for-tech-jobs/)6 月份的技术岗位总数为 48，993 个，同比下降 43.7%。失业人数创下历史新高。随着工作机会越来越少，失业者或求职者的压力越来越大，他们必须能够随时通过任何设备访问房源。

[Personforce，](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208231948/http://www.personforce.com/newcontent/home.html)一家为 VentureBeat，Business Insider，paidContent，Harvard Crimson，the Stanford Daily 和 TechCrunch 的 [CrunchBoard，](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208231948/http://www.crunchboard.com/jobs/)提供工作和分类网站的初创公司，正在推出一个移动工作平台，可以让任何工作板或职业网站将其网站转化为 iPhone，Android 或黑莓应用程序。

Personforce 已经推出了一款名为“高薪工作”的 iPhone 应用程序，带有 [Next Mobile Web。该应用程序列出了来自凯业必达公司 Dice.com 的职位。怪物和更多。基本上，Personforce 可以将任何职业网站转化为移动应用程序。Personforce 的首席执行官 Rohin Dar 表示，公司可能会发现这特别有吸引力，因为他们可以为内部工作数据库创建移动应用程序。CareerBuilder 已经有一个免费的 iPhone 应用程序，Beyond 也有一个，可以搜索工作网站和汇总列表。](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208231948/http://nextmobileweb.com/)

Personforce has already launched an iPhone app, called “High Paying Jobs,” with [Next Mobile Web.](https://web.archive.org/web/20221208231948/http://nextmobileweb.com/) The app lists jobs from Dice.com, CareerBuilder. Monster and more. Basically, Personforce can takes any career website and turns it into a mobile application. Personforce’s CEO Rohin Dar says that companies may find this particularly appealing, as they can create mobile apps for their internal job databases. CareerBuilder already has a free iPhone app, and Beyond also has one as well, which searches job sites and aggregates listings.
