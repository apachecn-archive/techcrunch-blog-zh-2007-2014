是时候开始抨击硅谷的[现实](https://web.archive.org/web/20230206031203/https://techcrunch.com/2008/10/10/an-ignoble-but-much-needed-end-to-web-20/)了。Seesmic 创始人 [Loic Le Meur](https://web.archive.org/web/20230206031203/http://www.crunchbase.com/person/loic-le-meur) 解雇了七名员工[他说](https://web.archive.org/web/20230206031203/http://www.loiclemeur.com/english/2008/10/tough-times-tou.html)，这超过了公司的三分之一。这是继几周前解雇三名员工之后的又一事件。
