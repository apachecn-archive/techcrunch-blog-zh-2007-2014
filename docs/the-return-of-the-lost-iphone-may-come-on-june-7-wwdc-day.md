# 丢失的 iPhone 可能会在 6 月 7 日 WWDC 日回归

![](img/b27d3649283186087d9d4c321cb0e3a0.png)

在所有的[警察突袭](https://web.archive.org/web/20221006104459/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2010/04/26/the-iphone-leak-gets-ugly-police-raid-gizmodo-editors-house-confiscate-computers/)和[法律思考](https://web.archive.org/web/20221006104459/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2010/04/26/iphone-leak-investigation-on-hold-as-da-ponders-gizmodo-shield-law-defense/)之后，苹果工程师 Gray Powell [在酒吧](https://web.archive.org/web/20221006104459/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2010/04/19/iphone-hd-4g/)丢失的下一代 iPhone 很可能会重新出现在苹果全球开发者大会上，该公司今天[宣布](https://web.archive.org/web/20221006104459/http://www.crunchgear.com/2010/04/28/huzzah-wwdc-2010-is-happening-on-june-7/)将于 6 月 7 日举行。人们普遍预计，苹果将在发布会上正式推出其下一代 iPhone，iPhone HD 或其他名称。

是的，这款手机有前置摄像头[用于视频聊天](https://web.archive.org/web/20221006104459/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2010/01/30/bigger-better-ipad-arc-computing/)，更大的电池，更好的麦克风，以及复古的 70 年代博朗风格。也许还会留下一些惊喜。

如果没有，那么总会有 iPad、 [iPhone OS 4.0](https://web.archive.org/web/20221006104459/http://www.mobilecrunch.com/2010/04/08/everything-you-need-to-know-about-iphone-os-4-0/) 、 [iAds](https://web.archive.org/web/20221006104459/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2010/04/08/apple-announces-iad-mobile-advertising-platform/) 和关于 Cocoa 的 API 设计的“技巧和诀窍”会议。
