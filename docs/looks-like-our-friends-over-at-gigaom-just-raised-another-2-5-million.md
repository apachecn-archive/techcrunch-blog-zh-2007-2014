# 看起来我们在 GigaOm 的朋友又筹集了 250 万美元

![](img/bc9be8333fc16aee9c0b863e0e24ab08.png)

[奥姆·马利克](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://www.crunchbase.com/person/om-malik)，备受尊敬的记者，科技新闻博客网 [Giga Omni Media](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://www.crunchbase.com/company/gigaomnimedia) 及其旗舰博客 [GigaOm](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://gigaom.com/) ，[在大约一个月前爆料了关于我们被 AOL](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://gigaom.com/2010/09/27/aol-close-to-buying-techcrunch/) 收购[的新闻](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2010/09/28/tim-armstrong-we-got-techcrunch/)。

我们今天正在报复他们，即使只是一点点:根据一份 [SEC 文件](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1503347/000150334710000001/xslFormDX01/primary_doc.xml)，看起来这家出版公司刚刚又筹集了 250 万美元，使 Giga Omni Media 筹集的资金总额达到了 833 万美元。

**更新:** GigaOm 写[“好吧，我们得到了更多的钱”](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://gigaom.com/2010/10/22/okay-we-got-more-money/)。你可以在那里获得更多关于公司状况、未来计划和最新雇员的详细信息。

最近一轮融资总额为 450 万美元，于两年前结束。

新的资金注入来自 [Alloy Ventures](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://www.crunchbase.com/financial-organization/alloy-ventures) 和[True Ventures](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://www.crunchbase.com/financial-organization/true-ventures)(Malik 是[合伙人](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/http://www.trueventures.com/om/)的硅谷早期风险投资公司)。

这发生在 GigaOm 刚刚失去资深作家兼 NewTeeVee 的自由编辑 Liz Gannes 的时候。

我们联系了 Om，请其对融资轮以及该公司计划如何处理这笔资金发表评论。更多的收购[更小的](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2008/09/15/om-is-hungry-buys-another-blog-theappleblog/) [博客](https://web.archive.org/web/20221005185428/https://beta.techcrunch.com/2008/07/22/gigaom-buys-a-mobile-blog-the-beginning-of-the-blog-rollup/)，或许？
