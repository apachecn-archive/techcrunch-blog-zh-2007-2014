哇哦。根据联合创始人兼合伙人保罗·格拉厄姆的推特[消息，位于硅谷的创业孵化器](https://web.archive.org/web/20230131013432/http://twitter.com/#!/paulg/status/123509842412445696) [Y Combinator](https://web.archive.org/web/20230131013432/http://ycombinator.com/) 每分钟收到一份申请。今天是申请下一期 Y Combinator 课程的最后一天，很明显，创始人们都急于递交申请。

早在八月份，格雷厄姆[说 Y Combinator 的录取率](https://web.archive.org/web/20230131013432/https://techcrunch.com/2011/08/23/y-combinator-demo-day-the-ultimate-roundup/)只有 3%。但是由于申请人数的增加，Y Combinator 最近增加了班级人数。

6 月，Graham [透露](https://web.archive.org/web/20230131013432/https://techcrunch.com/2011/06/01/paul-graham-total-value-of-y-combinator-funded-startups-is-4-7-billion/)YC 公司的总价值约为 30 亿美元。总的来说，YCombinator 已经资助了 316 家初创公司，包括 8 月份刚刚亮相的夏季融资。Checkout Graham 和 Y Combinator 合伙人 Harj Taggar 最近在 TechCrunch 与初创公司创始人的办公时间扰乱了这里的[。](https://web.archive.org/web/20230131013432/https://techcrunch.com/2011/09/12/tc-disrupt-office-hours-with-yc-partners-paul-graham-and-harj-taggar/)
